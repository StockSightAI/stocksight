# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Retail investors — primarily beginners and casual traders — researching stocks, indexes, and crypto. They arrive in short sessions (checking a ticker, reviewing their portfolio, practicing paper trades) on both desktop and mobile. Many are new to investing and need plain-English explanations rather than jargon.

## Product Purpose

StockSight is a free, no-paywall stock research platform: live TradingView charts, AI-powered buy/hold/sell analysis, price predictions, paper trading, portfolio tracking, and crypto — with no API key or account required to browse. Success looks like a beginner confidently understanding a stock in under a minute, and returning daily to check their watchlist and portfolio.

## Brand Personality

Calm and trustworthy. Professional restraint — the data and analysis speak, the chrome stays quiet. Approachable for beginners without being childish; serious without being intimidating.

## Anti-references

- AI-generated slop: generic purple gradients on everything, glow effects, graph-paper backgrounds, gradient text, template-grade layouts. The site has already been deliberately stripped of these — do not reintroduce them.
- Anything that makes a visitor think "a model generated this."

## Design Principles

1. **Restraint is credibility.** In finance, visual noise reads as untrustworthy. Flat colors, crisp borders, quiet chrome.
2. **Data first.** Charts, prices, and verdicts are the heroes; decoration never competes with them.
3. **Unseen details compound.** Press feedback, deliberate easing, consistent spacing — polish users feel but never notice.
4. **Beginner-readable.** Every number gets a plain-English explanation nearby; green/red always paired with text, never color alone.
5. **Fast feels free.** The product's pitch is "free and instant" — the UI must never feel heavier than the promise.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` (already implemented).
- Color is never the only signal for gain/loss — pair with +/− signs and text.
- Body text contrast ≥ 4.5:1 in both dark and light themes.
- Touch targets and 16px input font-size on mobile (already enforced to prevent iOS zoom).
