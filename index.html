Privacy Policy
Last updated: June 2026
1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. We also store your app preferences (dark/light mode, portfolio data, watchlists) locally in your browser and synced to our secure database.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to operate StockSight — to authenticate your account, sync your data across sessions, and send password reset emails when requested. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. We Do Not Sell Your Data

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS). Passwords are hashed using SHA-256 and never stored in plain text. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

5. Local Storage & Cookies

StockSight uses your browser's local storage to save your session and preferences. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

6. Third-Party Services

We use TradingView for chart data and Resend for transactional emails (password resets only). These services have their own privacy policies.

7. Account Deletion

You can permanently delete your account at any time from your profile settings. This removes all your data from our servers.

8. Financial Disclaimer

StockSight provides stock data and research tools for informational purposes only. Nothing on this platform constitutes financial, investment, or legal advice. Always consult a qualified professional before making investment decisions.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Email us at stocksightai@gmail.com.
